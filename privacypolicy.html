Privacy Policy

As a composite application product company, Cogility Software Corporation. ("Cogility") is committed to ensure privacy on the Internet. This policy covers the treatment of personally identifiable information collected by Cogility on its Web site. Cogility has adopted this policy to make sure the users' personal information remains confidential and secure.

The site does not currently use cookies. If cookies are implemented, they would be used solely to identify a user that has previously visited the site, and we would only pull the cookie for our site specifically.

Cogility will not sell or rent information gathered from users of this Web site to any third party without first obtaining the users' consent.

The site collects data through e-mail links that allow a user to request information about Cogility, its products and services or report problems with the Web site. For this purpose, we request and collect a user's contact information, consisting of name and e-mail address. The user may also choose to provide additional information. Cogility will only use the information to respond to the request of the user. The user's contact information may be used for mailings of such information by Cogility.

We may use user IP addresses to help diagnose problems with our server and administer our Web site, to analyze trends, to track user movement, and to gather broad demographic information. Such data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

The Cogility Web site may contains links to other Web sites. This privacy policy only applies to the Cogility Web site, and does not apply to any linked Web site or any other Web site. Users should read the policy posted on linked sites or contact that site's operator for information on their privacy policy.

Cogility may edit this policy from time to time. In the event of a policy change, Cogility will post the new policy on the Web site, and the prior policy will apply to any information collected before the modification.
