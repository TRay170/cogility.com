Provides products to commercial organizations and government projects seeking to improve their application delivery and development timelines and resource load

The Benefits of Cogility

Flexible and Organized

At the operational level Cogility Studio has great flexibility in maintaining application up-time, in coordinating the activities of all contributors to the operational system, and in dealing with the realities of maintaining a high availability and high throughput scaleable application running well.

Tools to Fit Your Business Needs

At the development level Cogility Studio provides modeling, runtime, and monitoring tools to reduce development costs, make the resulting system more understandable outside the development organization, and ensure coordination between development and operational groups through solid configuration management and automated deployment features. Cogility Studio relies on UML based models of the application to provide a holistic view of the application at all times. Developers remain in more in the domain space than the implementation space using Cogility Studio tools. The resulting models allow generation of views that can be presented to all stakeholders to disseminate the application's requirements, features, and behavior. All generated documentation and views are updated as model changes are produced allowing confidence in the shared view of the application.

A Framework That Scales

The Cogility Studio offering is focused on creation of composite applications (applications that integrate other applications and provide unique features and functions on top of those components). Cogility Software also offers frameworks built on top of Cogility Studio for specific verticals allowing projects to start faster and complete sooner. The frameworks embody base domain models, integrations to key data sources and applications, and integration with Web UI components to provide across the board support for a full scale application.
